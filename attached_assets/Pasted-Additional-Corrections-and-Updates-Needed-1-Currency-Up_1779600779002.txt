3. Correct Financial Formulas
   Please apply the following formulas correctly throughout the system:

* Non VAT Amount:
  Non VAT = Quantity × Unit Price

* Amount Before VAT (B4 VAT):
  B4 VAT = Quantity × Unit Price

* VAT:
  VAT = B4 VAT × 0.15

* Withhold Deduction:
  Withhold = B4 VAT × 0.03

* Withhold for Non VAT:
  Withhold for Non VAT = Non VAT × 0.03
